Why We Started

A journey from a question to a mission.

Driven By Purpose, Not Profit

Waseela Drug and Psychiatric Center was never built as a business. It was born from a realization that deeply moved Dr. Awais Khan and his medical team :

“If treatment centers exist… why is the cycle of addiction still not breaking?”

Everywhere they looked, the sector was crowded with facilities focusing on beds, billing, and business models. Almost no one was addressing the human being trapped behind the addiction—the deep-rooted trauma, the crushing loneliness, and the mental scars that drugs leave behind long before they destroy the body.

Instead of following the crowd, they searched for a deeper cure.

They spent months talking to families, meeting recovering addicts, visiting failed cases, and slowly one truth became clear:

After years of clinical experience and meeting broken families, one truth became clear:
People don't just need another "Rehab." They need a sanctuary that believes in their worth, even when they have lost faith in themselves.
